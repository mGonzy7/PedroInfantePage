Pedro Infante

El Idolo de Guamúchil

Facts about Pedro Infante:

  • Golden Age Icon – Pedro Infante was a key figure in Mexico’s Golden Age of Cinema (1930s–1950s), starring in over 60 films, including *Tizoc* (1957) and *Nosotros los Pobres* (1948).
  • Talented Singer – He was also a successful ranchera and bolero singer, recording more than 350 songs, including hits like *Cien años* and *Amorcito Corazón*.
  • Tragic Death – Infante, who was passionate about aviation, tragically died in a plane crash on April 15, 1957, in Mérida, Yucatán, at just 39 years old.
  • First Mexican Actor to Win a Berlinale Award – He won the Silver Bear for Best Actor at the Berlin International Film Festival in 1957 for *Tizoc*, a film he starred in alongside María Félix.
  • Still a Cultural Icon – Decades after his death, he remains a Mexican cultural legend, with fans still celebrating his legacy, especially on the anniversary of his passing.
